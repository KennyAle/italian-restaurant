About us

Restaurant was opened by award winning Chef Luciano Canova to offer residents and visitors of southern Brooklyn with the authentic Italian cuisine that the Restaurant's name has become synonymous with after the success of the Restaurant in Las Vegas. The restaurant now has a loyal following of its own, offering traditional Roman recipes passed down from Chef Luciano's family, as well as inventive Northern and Southern Italian preparations inspired by Chef Luciano's culinary education and travels.

Our Story

At restaurant, our story is one deeply rooted in family and a love for food. Our journey began generations ago in the charming villages of Italy, where recipes were passed down through the ages, preserving the essence of Italian cooking. Today, we bring these cherished recipes to your table, infusing each dish with the love and authenticity that makes Italian food so special.
